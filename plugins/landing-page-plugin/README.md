# Landing Page Plugin

Sales and marketing pages for products and lead capture.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| sales-page | Writes a long-form sales page with hooks, benefits, objections, and CTAs |
| product-page | Writes a product page focused on features, use cases, and positioning |
| opt-in-page | Writes a lead capture page for email signups and free offers |
