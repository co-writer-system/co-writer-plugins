# Social Plugin

Social media posts for LinkedIn and Substack Notes.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| linkedin-post | Writes LinkedIn posts with hooks, structure, and CTAs |
| substack-note | Writes short-form Substack Notes for engagement and reach |

## Connector

This plugin works standalone, but unlocks more when connected to **Substack**.

| Feature | Without Connector | With Connector |
|---------|------------------|----------------|
| Content angles | Writes from your brief and positioning | Checks which Notes got engagement to inform tone and topic |
| Repurposing | Repurposes content you provide manually | Pulls your recent published posts to generate Notes that drive traffic back |

**Setup:** Connect at app.alexmcfarland.ai/connectors
