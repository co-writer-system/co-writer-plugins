# Research Plugin

Structured, citation-backed research for any topic. Instead of doing research manually and pasting it into Claude, these skills run the full research process — pulling live sources, synthesizing findings, and delivering organized reports with citations. Covers everything from deep-dive reports to quick industry catch-ups to competitive landscape mapping.

**Requires connectors:** This plugin is built around the **Perplexity** and **Firecrawl** connectors. The skills technically work without them, but research quality drops significantly — you're limited to Claude's training data with no live web access. Set up both connectors at app.alexmcfarland.ai/connectors.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| deep-research | Comprehensive research report on any topic with citations and structured sections |
| competitive-analysis | Map a competitive landscape — players, positioning, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, gaps |
| content-research-brief | Pre-writing research: sources, stats, angles, and talking points for a piece you're about to write |
| audience-research | Deep dive into an audience or ICP — who they are, what they care about, where they spend time |
| industry-briefing | Quick catch-up on an industry — trends, players, recent moves, outlook |
| source-synthesis | Give it URLs, get a structured synthesis of themes, agreements, contradictions, and takeaways |
| company-research | Everything about a company or person — for sales calls, partnerships, podcast guest prep |
| trend-analysis | What's emerging in a space — early signals, shifting narratives, where things are heading |

## Connector

This plugin requires both the **Perplexity** and **Firecrawl** connectors for full functionality.

| Feature | Without Connectors | With Connectors |
|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| Source quality | Limited to Claude's training data — no live sources | Pulls current web sources, articles, data, and company sites |
| Citations | No external citations — general knowledge only | Full citations with URLs throughout every report |
| Competitive data | Can only discuss well-known competitors from training data | Scrapes competitor sites, maps their structure, pulls current pricing and positioning |
| Recency | Knowledge cutoff limits — can't see recent moves or trends | Live data on recent developments, launches, market shifts |

**Setup:** Connect both at app.alexmcfarland.ai/connectors
