# Email Plugin

Email marketing sequences for sales, launches, and nurture campaigns.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| sales-sequence | Writes a multi-email sales sequence for a product or offer |
| launch-sequence | Writes a launch email series with urgency and countdown structure |
| nurture-sequence | Writes a relationship-building sequence for warming up subscribers |

## Connector

This plugin works standalone, but unlocks more when connected to **Kit**.

| Feature | Without Connector | With Connector |
|---------|------------------|----------------|
| Sequence structure | Writes generic sequences based on your offer details | Pulls existing broadcast stats to model what subject lines and angles get opens |
| Audience targeting | Writes to a described audience segment | References your actual tag structure and subscriber segments for precise targeting |
| Performance awareness | No data on past email performance | Uses open rates, click rates, and broadcast history to improve each email |

**Setup:** Connect at app.alexmcfarland.ai/connectors
