# Ebook Plugin

Turn your expertise into downloadable ebooks — write from scratch, compile existing workspace content, or build lead magnets for email capture. Produces actual EPUB3 files using ebooklib (not just markdown), so your output is ready for distribution on any platform. Works in both Cowork and Claude Code.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| write-ebook | Writes a full ebook from scratch with outline approval, chapter drafting, and EPUB output |
| compile-ebook | Compiles existing workspace content (newsletters, articles, posts) into a structured ebook |
| lead-magnet | Creates a short, focused ebook designed for email capture and giveaways |
| ebook-ideator | Scans your workspace content and surfaces potential ebook compilations hiding in your existing work |

## Connector

No connector dependency. Works directly with markdown files in your workspace.

## Tip

Keep all your content as markdown files in your workspace for maximum portability and compilation flexibility.
