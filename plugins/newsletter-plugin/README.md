# Newsletter Plugin

Newsletter content in multiple formats for Substack and email platforms.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| thought-leadership | Writes opinionated essays that establish authority on a topic |
| how-to-guide | Writes step-by-step tutorial newsletters |
| story-lesson | Writes narrative-driven posts that extract a lesson from experience |
| case-study | Writes results-focused breakdowns of a specific project or outcome |
| launch-announcement | Writes product or feature launch newsletters |
| welcome-sequence | Writes onboarding emails for new subscribers |

## Connector

This plugin works standalone, but unlocks more when connected to **Substack**.

| Feature | Without Connector | With Connector |
|---------|------------------|----------------|
| Topic selection | Writes based on your brief and positioning | Checks which posts performed best to inform topic and angle |
| Format decisions | Uses the format you choose | Pulls email open rates and engagement data to recommend the right format |
| Audience awareness | Writes to your described audience | References subscriber growth trends and what content drives signups |

**Setup:** Connect at app.alexmcfarland.ai/connectors
